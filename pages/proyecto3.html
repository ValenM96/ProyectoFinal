SG Duke 84

In this project, my goal was to enhance my hard surface skills and create a game-ready asset. I worked on this project as part of the Blender Bros' Game Ready Asset course. Through this work, I aimed to refine my ability to model precise and functional details, focusing on optimizing performance and visual quality for use in games.

Modeling: Blender
Texturing: Substance Painter
Rendering: Blender (Cycles)
Composition: Photoshop
